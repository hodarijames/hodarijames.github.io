Art Direction Notes for Sully

  • Mood: Serene, hopeful, warm. The visual payoff after Concept 1.
  • Star: Same orange K dwarf, but calm. Smooth surface, no flares. Steady warm glow. Inviting, not threatening.
  • Planet: Earth-like but alien. Blue-green tones for oceans and vegetation. Intact atmosphere visible as a thin glowing rim.
  • Vegetation: Under K dwarf light, plants might trend darker green, red, or purple (the star puts out more red/infrared). Speculative but scientifically motivated.
  • Feeling: The viewer should think "life could thrive here." Warmth, stability, time.
  • Palette: Deep space blues/blacks. Star in warm amber/orange (softer than Concept 1). Planet in blue-greens with alien vegetation hints. Overall warmer and more inviting.
  • No text or labels needed.
  • Dimensions: Same as Concept 1: ~10" × 12" at 300 dpi (3000 × 3600 px). PNG, dark background.
