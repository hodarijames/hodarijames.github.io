Art Direction Notes for Sully

  • Mood: Hostile, dramatic, energetic. This star is young and angry.
  • Star: Orange K dwarf (cooler than our Sun, but NOT red like an M dwarf). Show visible surface turbulence, prominences (magnetic loops arching off the surface), and flare jets.
  • Prominences: Arching loops of plasma rising from the stellar surface and curving back down. These are driven by magnetic field activity and are a real feature of active stars.
  • Planet: Rocky, barren, no life. Atmosphere visibly being stripped away by stellar wind. Surface with craters or volcanic activity.
  • UV/radiation: Visual cue of intense radiation bathing the planet. Could be purple/UV glow, particle streams, or artistic interpretation.
  • Palette: Deep space blues/blacks. Star in oranges/ambers. Purple/violet accents for UV. Planet feels dry and lifeless.
  • No text or labels needed.
  • Dimensions: ~10" × 12" at 300 dpi (3000 × 3600 px). PNG, dark background.
